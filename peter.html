Peter Felix

Felix Peter is an entrepreneur-philanthropist. Felix Peter is committed to contributing to the society through Peter Felix Foundation- a Non Government Organization whose primary objective is to empower people in slums to optimize their potential by leveraging relevant information resources. He is a dynamic Teacher and Inspirational speaker. His mission is to inspire his generation to maximize their strengths, unlock their true potentials, and lead purposeful and impactful lives. He represents a generation that believes that you can be empowered in all areas regardless of your background, colour and environment. He strongly believes we all are drivers of our destiny. He is an advocate for people in slums, mostly in Nigeria. Health and education is major concern for people in slums and with Peter Felix Foundation focus on health and education, hope is being guaranty for the hopeless through this efforts. He is also an inspirational speaker and author. His messages have transformed many lives, business, and organizations within and outside the country. He is a much sort after speaker. He has trained many young leaders through his leadership platform-The Journey Begins. He is a fan of self-development with numerous leadership certificates (Daystar Basic and Advance Leadership Academy, Lead Cap Alliance, Global Leadership Summit, etc)
