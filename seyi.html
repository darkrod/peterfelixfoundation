SEYI ALAWUH

Seyi Alawuh is a visual story teller who uses his images as a tool of positive change and solutions. Seyi works currently as an Associate Photographer with SocialGood Lagos - a partner of the United Nations towards achieving the SDGs vision 2030. Seyi has been in the Photography creative industry for 8 years but became a full time Photographer 2 years ago gathering a wealth of experience in the industry. He also owns a Photography company called Bezaleel Photography - a brand that provides quality service and preserve memories through motion and still images. Seyi has some training with Canon, Shaw Academy Dublin Ireland, SB Visuals to mention a few. Seyi is open to training and learning as long as it improves creativity and help build positive relationships.
