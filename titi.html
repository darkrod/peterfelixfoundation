ASAOLU TITILOPE

Asaolu, Titilope Mercy is a registered Nurse by profession, BLS, CPR & AED, Bloodborne Pathogens certified, also, An American Cancer Society Treat the Pain Personnel. A Writer and Speaker and a business woman. Titilope currently works with Vivayla Medical centre, Ibafo, Ogun state. She has volunteered for various health outreaches and projects including Divine Missions health outreach, Sebeccly Cancer Support and Lofe-Lofe health outreach. She has a passion for the girl child and longs to see them live out their full potentials
