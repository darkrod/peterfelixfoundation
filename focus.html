OUR FOCUS

HEALTH

One of the major problems of slum dwellers is poor health. We have made it our major priority to carry out project that will lead to sound health of slum dwellers.

EDUCATION

Education is one of the major Challenges of Slum Dwellers. We have it as one of our core project to support slum dwellers back to school.

TRAINING

Training is key in development of organizations, countries etc. We make it our priority to train staffs in schools, train volunteers for better performance and productivity.

VOLUNTEER

We don't only carry out project in Peter Felix Foundation, We also help other NGOs make their dreams come true, through financial and human resources.
