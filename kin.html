Kingsley Ahanonu

Kingsley Ahanonu is a Health Safety and Environment Professional. He is also a Solar Energy Advocate and Entrepreneur whose passion for training led him to train over 30 NYSC members in Lagos and Ogun State on solar energy design and installation. He holds a Degree in Microbiology from Michael Opkara University, Abia State. He is a holder of various international certifications including; UK HSE level 1, 2 & 3, PV Installer from Canadian Solar, Lead Auditor/Implementer on ISO 45001:2015 and Lean Six Sigma Yellow Belt professional. As a young leader who has interest in humanity, he was the president of Gender Vanguard Community Development Service during his NYSC days. He led the group on gender equality awareness programs in communities and schools in Obafemi Owode L.G.A, Ogun State. As climate change advocate, he is a member of Research and Development Team of International Climate Change Development Initiative Africa. Also he teaches children in schools and communities upcycling skills to create awareness on plastic pollution and help achieve a safe and clean environment. As an advocate of health and safety he continuously creates safety awareness in schools, church and public places.
