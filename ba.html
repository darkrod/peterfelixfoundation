Barinaada Joy Alex

Barinaada Joy Bema is a lawyer,a writer and a business consultant. She provides legal advisory services to business owners. She is the founder of Barinaada a specialized law firm that is poised to work with startups, Micro Small and Medium Scale Enterprises and fast growing business organizations as they pass through rough times and journey through nothing and then finally evolve into gigantic businesses. Barinaada has a passion for small business owners. As a serial entrepreneur she has had over seven (7) years business experience, having done several businesses in the paston her own. She has now decided to channel her energy and expertise towards helping start-ups, MSMEs and Individuals with legal advisory service for their businesses with a view to ensuring that they maintain regulatory compliance as well as gain more insights and clarity about the legal aspect of their businesses. To this end she has successfully “birthed” several companies for her clients. Her ebook "Quick Guide to Business Registration in Nigeria" is a practical guide which literarily teaches you how to start and complete your business registration with the CAC on your own from the comfort of your home. In the book you would also learn how to choose the right business structure suitable to your kind of business. Barinaada graduated from the University of Lagos where she studied law then proceeded to the Nigerian Law School Enugu Campus after which she was called to the Nigerian Bar, having passed the Bar examinations in one sitting. She is a member of the Nigerian Bar Association and a member of the Nigerian Association of Adventist Lawyers (NAAL). Barinaada has gained some post bar work experiences including working at the former Law Office Of Abimbola Akerededolu (SAN) as an Associate and later at Peak Shipping Agency Ltd as a Company Secretary/Legal Adviser.She was an extern at Ayoola Julius & Co. during the mandatory externship organized by the Nigerian Law School and also acted as a research assistant to Professor Joke Oyewunmi on intellectual property Law at the University of Lagos. Barinaada is currently running her postgraduate programme (LLM) at the University of Lagos. Barinaada is the founder of LegalHub, a closed Facebook group with over 600 members where she shares her thoughts on corporate law practices and legal compliance issues as they affect small businesses as well as the legalchallenges facing business owners and possible way out. She works with business owners and helps them refine their ideas and business plans into thriving businesses. She has designed and executed various trainings on building legally protected businesses both online and offline. She is the go to person when it comes to gaining clarity about the legal aspect of your business.
