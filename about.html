About Us

Peter Felix foundation is an NGO based in Lagos, Nigeria. The NGO was birthed November 21st, 2017 with a vision of recreating slum and giving lives a meaning. The Organization reaches out to slum dwellers through health, educational and vocational project. The Organization distributed more than 300 mosquito nets and free medication in Makoko Community (One of the largest floating city in the world), and also send more than 200 children in Makoko Community to school, with free school uniforms, bags, books, pen, pencil. The Organization is governed by six board of Advisors, eight Executives and more than one hundred volunteers across Nigeria.
